About Us

Two best friends, who loved food and wanted to see other people enjoy their food, came together. These two friends came from two different countries. One was from India, and the other from South Korea. One day they decided to try fusing both cuisines, and they discovered that they were able to cook amazing delicious dishes. Therefore, they wanted to show case to the rest of the world how tasty a fusion between Indian and Korean could be! Hence, they started this Ko-Indian Kingdom to make it accessible for everyone!
